Ruby Radwan

LICENSED HYPNO PSYCHOTHERAPIST

I am a non-judgemental, supportive therapist

I understand that it is difficult to reach out for help. Daunting to share your problems and fears honestly with someone else. I have trained to be that person who can share your problems in order to help you discover your true inner strength. I can help enable you to carry on with confidence, to make the changes necessary to give you real hope, self value and confidence to change whatever you feel needs changing in your life.

I am an integrative psychotherapist

Also, I trained in therapeutic hypnotherapy. This places many useful techniques at my fingertips, so I am able to focus on your unique needs and requirements, rather than fit you into an academic theoretical approach. I believe in a holistic approach to life, health and wellbeing. I believe the mind and body are so intricately and elegantly linked that healing can happen when you take the very first step.

I am a human being

These responsibilities are based on the principle that I am a human being, worthy of respect, and that I am working to support my family through helping others to live better, happier and more fulfilling lives. By agreeing to work with me means taking certain responsibilities. These include treating me like a human being worthy of respect and an awareness that I have chosen work where I help others to live better, happier and more fulfilling lives.
